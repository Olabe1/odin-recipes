Description

These fluffy pancakes are soft on the inside and lightly golden on the outside. They are quick to make using simple ingredients you probably already have at home. Perfect for breakfast or brunch, these pancakes rise nicely on the pan and taste great with syrup, fruit, or a little butter.

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 3½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1¼ cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter (or oil)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix the flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt until combined.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and gently stir until just combined. Do not overmix small lumps are okay.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and lightly grease it with butter or oil.
  5. Pour about ¼ cup of batter onto the pan for each pancake.
  6. Cook until bubbles appear on the surface and the edges look set, then flip the pancake.
  7. Cook the other side until golden brown and cooked through.
  8. Remove from the pan and repeat with the remaining batter. Serve warm.
