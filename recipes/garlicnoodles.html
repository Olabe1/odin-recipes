Description

Garlic noodles are a quick and flavorful dish made with simple ingredients like garlic, soy sause, and noodles.

They are savory, slightly sweet, and perfect as a main dish or side.

This recipe is easy to prepare and ideal for busy weeknights when you want something delicious without spending too much time in the kitchen.

Ingredients

For the Secret Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 teaspoons fish sauce
  • ¼ teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper

For the Noodles:

  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 8 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 ounces spaghetti
  • ¼ cup finely grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese
  • 1 tablespoon chopped green onion, or to taste
  • 1 pinch red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, combine soy sause, oyster sause, Worcestershire sause, fish sauce, sesame oil, and cayenne pepper. Mix well and set aside.
  2. Heat butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook while stirring until fragrant, about one minute. Pour in the prepared sauce and remove the pen from heat.
  3. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente, then drain.
  4. Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet. Toss everything together, adding a little pasta water if needed to loosen the sauce. Mix in the parmesan cheese.
  5. Serve immediately and top with chopped green onions and red pepper flakes.
